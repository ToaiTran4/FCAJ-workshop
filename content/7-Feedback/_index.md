### General Assessment

**1. Work environment**
The work environment is very friendly and open. FCAJ members are always ready to help when I encounter difficulties, even outside working hours. The workspace is neat and comfortable, helping me focus better. However, I think we could add more networking sessions or team bonding activities to help everyone get to know each other better.

**2. Support from mentor / team admin**
Mentor guides very detailed, explains clearly when I don't understand and always encourages me to ask questions. Team admin supports procedures, documents and creates conditions for me to work conveniently. I highly appreciate that the mentor allows me to try and handle problems myself instead of just giving answers.

**3. Suitability between work and major**
The work assigned to me is suitable with the knowledge I learned at school, while expanding new areas I have never approached before. Thanks to that, I both consolidate my foundational knowledge and learn new practical skills.

**4. Learning opportunities & skill development**
During the internship, I learned many new skills like using project management tools, teamwork skills, and even how to communicate professionally in a company environment. Mentor also shares a lot of practical experience helping me orient my career better.

**5. Culture & team spirit**
Company culture is very positive: everyone respects each other, works seriously but still happily. When there is an urgent project, everyone tries together, supports regardless of position. This helps me feel like I'm part of the team, even just an intern.

**6. Policies / benefits for interns**
The company supports internship allowance and creates flexible time conditions when necessary. In addition, being able to participate in internal training sessions is a big plus.
